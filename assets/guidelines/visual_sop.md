# 📊 SupraWall Visual SOP: YouTube-Style Technical Strikes

To be followed for ALL LinkedIn multimedia broadcasts.

## 1. HUMAN FACE REQUIREMENTS
- **Dominant Subject**: One close-cropped face, taking up 40-60% of the frame.
- **Identity**: Model after Alejandro Paris (sharp features, short dark hair, slight stubble).
- **Expression**: Intense focus, shock/alarm, confident authority, or deep concern.
- **Lighting**: Cinematic/Dramatic (Rim light: Purple/Blue/White). Subtle screen glow (Green/Red) on face.
- **Background**: Flat black or very dark tech texture.

## 2. HEADLINE TEXT REQUIREMENTS
- **Payload**: 5-6 words MAXIMUM.
- **Tone**: Warning or Revelation.
- **Typography**: Ultra-bold condensed sans-serif (Bebas Neue / Anton / Impact).
- **Color**: Split color (Neon Green/Red for keywords, White for balance).
- **Placement**: Left or Bottom (Balanced with face).

## 3. COMPOSITION (1280x720)
- **Style**: High-contrast, "MrBeast" urgency.
- **Rule of Thirds**: Face on one side, text on the other.
- **Element Limit**: Max 3 elements (Face + Headline + 1 Accent Graphic).
